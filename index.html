Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 mins

Serving Size: 6

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients.
  3. Add milk and oil, stir until smooth.
  4. Pour into baking tin.
  5. Bake for 35 mins.
  6. Let it cool before serving.
